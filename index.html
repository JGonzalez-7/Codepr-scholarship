Holberton Scholarship Fund

The Holberton Scholarship Fund was established at the Fundación Comunitaria de Puerto Rico (Puerto Rico Community Foundation) in 2020 by Holberton Coding School, to provide complementary scholarships to students who due to their financial situation need supplemental financing to pursue the career of their dreams.

It holds the 1101.01 status from the Puerto Rico Internal Revenue Code, and the 501 (c) (3) certifications from the United States Internal Revenue Code.
